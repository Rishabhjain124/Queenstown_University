About Queenstown University

Located in the scenic heart of Queenstown, New Zealand, Queenstown University is a vibrant institution known for academic innovation, multicultural learning, and commitment to real-world education. We offer a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to meet global standards.

History

Established in 2002, Queenstown University started as a local institute with a vision to bring world-class education to one of the most beautiful regions in New Zealand. Today, it stands as a symbol of academic excellence with a strong focus on sustainability, technology, and global engagement.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission: To provide transformative education that empowers students to shape a better world.

Our Vision: To be a leading global university recognised for innovation, inclusivity, and impact.
