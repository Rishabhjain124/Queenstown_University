International Programs

Step into a world of global opportunity with Queenstown University's International Exchange Programs. We offer semester-long and year-long placements at renowned partner universities in Australia, Canada, and the UK — all with transfer-friendly credits. You can also enroll in dual-degree options that give you a competitive edge across continents. Through funded research exchanges, cultural immersion experiences, and mentorship from global faculty, our programs help you not only learn — but lead — anywhere in the world. Your global future begins here.
