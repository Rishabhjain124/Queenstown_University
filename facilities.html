Our Facilities

Modern Library

Our spacious, state-of-the-art library features over 50,000 physical books, extensive digital collections, academic journals, and research databases. Comfortable study zones, quiet rooms, group collaboration spaces, and digital research labs create an ideal environment for focused learning and innovation.

Advanced Computer Labs

Equipped with the latest PCs and Macs, industry-standard software, and high-speed internet access, our labs support courses in computer science, digital media, engineering, and data analytics. Labs are open 24/7 with expert technical support and help desks.

Sports & Fitness Complex

The expansive sports complex includes indoor courts for basketball, volleyball, and badminton, a fully equipped gymnasium, swimming pool, fitness studios for yoga and dance, and outdoor fields for rugby, cricket, soccer, and athletics. Regular fitness classes and sports clubs encourage a healthy, active lifestyle.

On-Campus Accommodation

Comfortable and secure housing options with furnished single and shared rooms, study lounges, communal kitchens, laundry facilities, and 24-hour security. Accommodation is conveniently located close to classrooms and campus amenities.

Research & Innovation Centers

Dedicated centers for environmental studies, business innovation, artificial intelligence, and sustainable development foster collaborative projects between students, faculty, and industry partners. Access to cutting-edge labs, funding opportunities, and expert mentorship supports groundbreaking research.

Student Support & Counseling Services

Comprehensive support services including academic advising, mental health counseling, career guidance, and peer mentoring programs help students navigate university life and plan their futures successfully.

Cultural & Performing Arts Center

A vibrant hub for theatre, music, dance, and art exhibitions. Facilities include rehearsal studios, performance theaters, art galleries, and workshops encouraging creative expression and cultural engagement.

Language & Learning Labs

Specialized labs equipped with multimedia tools for language learning, writing support, and study skills workshops. Tailored sessions enhance academic writing, pronunciation, and communication for international and local students.

Innovation & Entrepreneurship Hub

A dynamic space where aspiring entrepreneurs access mentorship, co-working areas, startup funding advice, and workshops on business planning, marketing, and pitching. Regular networking events connect students with industry leaders and investors.

Health & Wellness Center

On-campus clinic providing general medical services, health screenings, vaccination programs, and wellness workshops focusing on nutrition, stress management, and lifestyle improvement.

Transportation & Parking

Convenient shuttle services connect campus with the city center and key local areas. Ample parking spaces for students and staff with secure bike storage and charging stations for electric vehicles.
